Getting Started

This page will help you create your first online database App with built-in chat functionality on Bappo.

Create an account

Go to https://go.bappo.com and sign up. Once you've registered and verified your email, log in and you will see the screen below:

Create your first App

Click on Create New App and enter your App name.

Click Go and your App will be ready instantly!
